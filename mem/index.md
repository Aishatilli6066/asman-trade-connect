## Core
Brand palette ONLY: Deep Burgundy #7B1C2A, Rich Black #0D0D0D, Warm Gold #E8D5A3, White #FFFFFF. No other colors.
Gold (#E8D5A3) is an accent ONLY — buttons, accents, icons, dividers, hover effects, highlights. Never large fills/backgrounds. Do not overuse.
Tokens in src/styles.css: --color-burgundy, --color-ink, --color-gold, --color-gold-soft, --color-bone (#FAF8F3 off-white), --color-line. Use semantic tokens, not raw hex.
